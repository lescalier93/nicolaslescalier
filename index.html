About Me

I’m a Toronto-based broker with over 5 years of experience arranging Merchant Cash Advances, Term Loans, and Commercial Real Estate refinancing. I help businesses secure fast, flexible capital to optimize cash flow, fund growth, and manage construction projects.

Why choose me? I leverage deep relationships with multiple lenders, fast turnaround times, and personalized service to match you with the best financing solution for your unique needs.

  • Term Loan Structuring
