How to interpret the result

Lower total payback is not enough

A cheaper-looking offer can still create more weekly or daily stress if the term is compressed.

Cash flow fit matters most

The better structure is usually the one the business can absorb without destabilizing payroll, inventory, or vendor rhythm.

Use this as a filter, not a final decision

Real offer quality still depends on documentation, remittance mechanics, renewal pressure, and whether MCA is even the right product.
