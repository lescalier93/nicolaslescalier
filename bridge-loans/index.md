# Bridge Loans

Bridge financing offers immediate capital to seize opportunities while you arrange permanent funding or await another financial event. These loans help your business act quickly without missing opportunities.

**Who it’s for:** Businesses needing interim capital for acquisitions, renovations, or to cover temporary cash flow gaps  
**Collateral:** Usually secured by real estate or other hard assets  
**Terms & rates:** 3‑24 months with interest-only payments
