About Nicolas Lescalier

Nicolas Lescalier is a Canadian business financing broker who specializes in arranging Merchant Cash Advances (MCAs), term loans and commercial real estate (CRE) refinancing for entrepreneurs across the United States and Canada. He manages relationships with more than one hundred private lenders to ensure clients receive the most competitive funding options and fast approvals.

With over five years of experience in alternative lending, Nicolas has helped countless businesses improve cash flow, finance growth and bridge short‑term gaps. He serves clients ranging from new small businesses to established mid‑market companies and understands the unique financing needs of industries such as retail, construction, transportation and professional services.

When he’s not working on financing deals, Nicolas can be found following football (soccer) and reading about history. He believes that a well‑rounded perspective—gained through travel, sports and learning—is essential to understanding his clients’ challenges.
