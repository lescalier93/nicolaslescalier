# About Nicolas Lescalier — U.S. Business Financing Specialist

I’m **Nicolas Lescalier**, a funding manager dedicated to helping U.S. entrepreneurs secure the capital they need to succeed.  Born in France and based in Toronto, I spent my early career in commercial finance before focusing on alternative lending for small and mid‑sized businesses.  I bridge the gap between Canadian efficiency and U.S. market know‑how, offering clients the best of both worlds.

## My journey

My passion for business finance began at university, where I studied economics and realized how access to capital can transform lives.  After graduation I worked as a broker at a Manhattan‑based merchant cash advance company, gaining firsthand experience in the U.S. lending landscape.  In 2020 I founded my own consultancy, helping businesses obtain funding through MCAs, term loans, bridge loans and commercial real estate financing.

## Why I focus on U.S. businesses

The United States is home to millions of entrepreneurs with incredible ideas and drive.  Yet many are underserved by traditional banks.  The 2008 financial crisis tightened credit standards, and recent economic turbulence has made banks even more conservative.  By focusing on the U.S. market, I can offer alternative financing solutions that empower entrepreneurs from New York to California to pursue growth without unnecessary delays.
